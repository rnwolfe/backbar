## Acceptance

- [ ] The sweep shows current progress through the filtered bottle list.
- [ ] The operator can skip a bottle without recording a reading and continue to the next bottle.
- [ ] If a save fails, the flow stays on the same bottle, shows a retry path, and does not advance silently.
- [ ] A completed or skipped bottle is not double-submitted by repeated taps during an in-flight save.

## Notes

Emitted by feature plan si2b4s6g: "A rapid inventory update flow. The user rapidly goes through their bottles and selects fill level. Large buttons for quick tap. Immediately save and proceed to next bottle. On top of fractional fill levels, have a large button for empty/gone and continue."
